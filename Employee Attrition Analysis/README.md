## üìä Key Insights
| Observation | Business Insight |
|--------------|------------------|
| High attrition among employees with overtime | Indicates **workload management issue** |
| Lower satisfaction in Sales roles | Suggests **need for incentive or recognition programs** |
| Employees with <2 years experience leave most | HR should **focus on early engagement programs** |
| Mid-level income group shows highest attrition | Possible **market pay gap** issue |
| Work-life balance strongly correlates with attrition | Reinforces **flexibility and hybrid work importance** |
